Working Group Prototype

Morris Township Planning Map

A working map for reviewing walkability and bikeability concerns in Morris Township, with current hotspot records organized for discussion of sidewalk gaps, crossings, and local destinations.

This prototype presents TAC source material as a clearer civic reference: one map for orientation, one working rail for filtering records, and one place to review what each location represents.
